### Popular use cases

- **DeFi Application:** Provide DeFi services with social recovery, spending limits, and automated transaction execution.
- **Financial Services:** Offer access to 24/7 global crypto markets, all without crypto on the balance sheet.
- **AI Agent:** Deploy programmable wallets designed for AI with execution, automation, and security controls.
- **Consumer Social App:** Enable users to mint and trade social tokens without ever seeing a blockchain transaction or gas fee.
- **Gaming Platform:** Players can earn, trade, and use in-game assets across multiple games without managing wallets.
